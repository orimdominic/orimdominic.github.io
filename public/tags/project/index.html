    @PickAtRandom Twitter Bot

    A Twitter user may decide to run a giveaway for a random selection of Twitter users who interacted with a tweet of theirs. @mykeels saw a possibility to automate this and created a Random-Twitter-Bot specification. Twitter users should not have to go through the stress of perusing their tweet interactors to select winners of a random giveaway. He suggested the idea to me and I built it. In this article, I share my experience building @PickAtRandom for anyone desiring to build something similar....
